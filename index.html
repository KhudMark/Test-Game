How to Play

Use arrow keys (desktop) or swipe (mobile) to slide all tiles.

When two tiles showing the same player collide, they merge into a more obscure player at the next tier.

Famous players like LeBron James are common and appear with small values (2, 4). Keep merging to unlock increasingly obscure players — all the way to the 2048 tile!

Each day features a new set of NBA players. New players unlock at midnight.
