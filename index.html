How to Play

Arrow keys or swipe to slide. Two identical tiles merge into the next tier.

Start with global icons (LeBron, Curry = 2 tile). Merge them to unlock increasingly obscure players up to the 2048 tile. No names shown — test your eye!

Each time you reach a new tile value, a popup asks you to name the player. Correct answers earn bonus power-ups.

↩ Undo — revert last move  |  💣 Bomb (unlocks at 64) — remove any tile  |  ⚡ Double (unlocks at 256) — make 2 moves before a new tile spawns.
